FAQ

Common questions

The AI Assistant is trained on landlord-tenant law, Fair Housing guidelines, maintenance triage, lease management, and property investment principles. It can help with late rent situations, lease break requests, Fair Housing questions, maintenance decisions, rent pricing, vendor communications, eviction process guidance, and general property management questions. It provides general guidance — not legal advice — and always recommends consulting a local attorney for complex legal matters.
RentGuard's screening module applies consistent, documented criteria to every applicant — income ratio, credit score, rental history, and employment stability. The system explicitly excludes any protected class information and reminds you at every step to apply identical standards. You enter the financial and rental history data; the AI scores it against your defined criteria. This creates an auditable, consistent screening record that demonstrates equal treatment.
RentGuard is an AI intelligence layer — it handles the decision-making, document generation, and analysis work. It doesn't currently process rent payments or handle accounting in the way dedicated platforms like Baselane or TurboTenant do. Most landlords use RentGuard alongside a payment processor. Many find they can drop their existing PM software entirely once they see how much RentGuard handles.

No app store required, no download needed, no storage consumed.
